Guide

Welcome to Haikyuu!! Touch The Dream! This guide is for both new and returning players.

  • Download & Install: Find the official game on your app store for the Southeast Asian region (Japan version or SEA version).
  • Note: If you are outside of Japan or the SEA region, you can use QooApp (Android/iOS) or create a Play Store account for Japan or the SEA region Watch Here
  • Reroll Account: Play the tutorial for two to three minutes. Then, go to Settings and click on Delete Account.
  • Claim Rewards: Check your mailbox for pre-registration and daily rewards. for japan version go to this official haikyuu website for redeem code if available.

Gameplay Tips

  • Build a Balanced Team: Mix attackers, defenders, and Coach characters.
  • Level Up Key Players: Invest in your main lineup first.
  • Participate in Events: Daily events and Limited-time events offer unique rewards.
  • Join a Club: Get bonuses buff and interact with other players.
  • Advice for Story Quest: For now, only five teams always appear in the story quest: Karasuno, Nekoma, Aoba Josai, Date Tech, and Shiratorizawa. Focus on leveling up players from these teams if you want to clear all the story quests. At least four players from each team should have a player type buff (legend or iconic) and players with the "Rock," "Scissors," and "Paper" classes.
